## ğŸ“– Background

The Fermi Paradox, named after physicist Enrico Fermi, asks: Given the billions of stars and potentially habitable planets in our galaxy, why haven't we detected any signs of alien civilizations?

The **Great Filter** hypothesis suggests there's some extremely difficult step in the evolution from primordial soup to galaxy-spanning civilization. The question is: where is that filter, and have we already passed it?

This game explores 50 different proposed filters from scientific literature, science fiction, and philosophical speculation.
